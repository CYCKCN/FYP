how it works

Step 1: Create an account with your ITSC account

First things first, you'll need to create an account on our platform using your ITSC account credentials. This ensures that only HKUST students can use our platform and keeps it safe and secure.

Step 2: List your products

Once you're logged in, it's time to start listing the products you want to sell. Take a picture of your items and input all the necessary information, like the product name, description, price, and condition. You can even categorize your products to make it easier for other students to find them.

Step 3: Post your items!

Once you've listed your products, it's time to post them on our platform for other students to see. Make sure all the information is accurate and up-to-date to avoid any misunderstandings or disputes.

Step 4: Browse and find your new favorite things

Now it's time to start browsing the products listed on our platform. You can search for products by category, brand, or keyword. You'll be able to view product details like price, description, and images, as well as reviews from other students. Who knows, you might just find your new favorite thing on our platform!

Step 5: Communicate with the sellers

When you find a product you want to buy, you can communicate with the seller through our messaging system. This way, you can ask any questions you might have about the product, negotiate the price, or arrange a meet-up.

Step 6: Meet up and exchange your goods

Once you and the seller have agreed on the terms of the transaction, it's time to meet up and exchange the product and payment. We recommend meeting in a public place on campus, like a library or caf√©, to ensure your safety and security.
